◆ TAG_DFDE01_KERNEL_PRIVATE_NON_BER

#define TAG_DFDE01_KERNEL_PRIVATE_NON_BER   0xDFDE01

For enclosing private kernel data with tags that are non-BER-TLV-compliant returned by EMV_CTLS_fetchTxnTags(). In case of multiple of these tags, multiple 'DFDE01' are returned. The pay-load data is preceded by two byte tag number and two byte pay-load data length.
