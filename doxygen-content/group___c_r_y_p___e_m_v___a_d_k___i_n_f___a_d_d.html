Detailed Description

9F27 (cryptogram information) can carry more information than just AAC, TC, and ARQC. The ICC has the possibility to give back these additional information. Calling application may use these defines to analyse EMV_CT_TRANSRES_STRUCT::T_9F27_CryptInfo.
