◆ CTS_OPTION_TAG_UX_MSR_TIMEOUT

#define CTS_OPTION_TAG_UX_MSR_TIMEOUT   0xDFDB42

Ux30x only: How long to wait for MSR data after card removal.
Precondition: Callback CTS_NOTIFICATION_CBK_TYPE_UX_CARD_INSERTED is registered.
Format b2 (binary coded, little endian). min = 0x0001, max = 0xFFFF = 65535 ms.
"0x0000" means "wait until main timeout of cts_StartSelection() expires"
